Securing Personal Identity in Facial Recognition: The Shift to Machine Unlearning

Co-Authors: Uday Kiran G, Srilakshmi V, Moulika B, Laukya G, Ruthick M

Published in: 4th International Conference on Sustainable Expert Systems (ICSES) by IEEE, 2024

Abstract: The project explores advanced machine unlearning techniques using MUFAC and MUCAC datasets to enhance privacy and compliance in facial recognition. It addresses trade-offs between data protection and model accuracy while developing privacy-preserving machine unlearning methods that maintain model utility.

Integrating NAS for Human Pose Estimation

Co-Authors: Uday Kiran G, Srilakshmi V, Moulika B, Laukya G, Ruthick M

Published in: International Conference on Evolutionary Computing and Mobile Sustainable Networks (ICECMSN) by Science direct, 2024

Abstract: Pose Neural Fabric Search (PoseNFS) introduces a novel NAS framework leveraging domain-specific knowledge for human pose estimation.It optimizes part-specific architectures, achieving superior accuracy and computational efficiency on complex datasets by applying Cell-based Neural Fabric (CNF) and advanced NAS for keypoint detection
